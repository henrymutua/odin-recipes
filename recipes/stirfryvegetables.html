"Stir fry vegetables recipe"

"Description"

"Easy Vegetable Stir Fry is a mixture of colorful vegetables sautéed in a sweet and savory sauce that makes for a simple weeknight meal! Less than 30 minutes to make from start to finish!"

"Ingredients"

  • "Olive Oil"
  • "Vegetables: red bell pepper, yellow bell peppers, sugar snap peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, baby corn, water chestnuts."
  • "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  • "Garnish: green onions and sesame seeds"

"Steps"

  1. "Sauté: In a wok or large skillet add 1 Tablespoon olive oil over medium high heat. Add bell pepper, peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, baby corn, and water chestnuts. Sauté 2-3 minutes until veggies are almost tender."
  2. "Whisk: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth, and cornstarch."
  3. "Mix: Pour over veggies and cook until the sauce has thickened."
  4. "Salt slow."
  5. "Garnish: add chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired."
