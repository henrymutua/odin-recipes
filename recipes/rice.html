"Rice recipe"

"Description"

"You don't need a rice cooker to make foolproof rice. Use this recipe to make basic white rice in a saucepan on the stovetop. It's easy and will come out right every time if you follow a few simple steps."

"Ingredients"

  • "1 cup long-grain white rice"
  • "2 cups water"
  • "1/2 teaspoon salt"

"Steps"

  1. "In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the salt, stir, and then add the rinsed and drained rice. Stir with a fork."
  2. "Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 20 minutes. Check it after 15 minutes to see if all the water has evaporated. If it has, the rice is ready. If not, replace the lid and let the rice simmer an additional 5 minutes."
  3. "Remove from the heat and fluff with a fork. Transfer to a plate and serve garnished with butter and parsley, if desired."
